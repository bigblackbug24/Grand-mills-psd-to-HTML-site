Apricot Loaf

Preheat oven to 170"C. Grease a large loaf pan and line with baking paper

Ingredients
  • 250g dried apricots, chopped
  • ½ cup apricot nectar
  • 1 cup light brown sugar
  • 225g butter, chopped
  • 1 ¾ cups wholemeal flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 3 eggs, lightly beaten
How to make

Preheat oven to 170°C. Grease a large loaf pan and line with baking paper.
Place apricots, nectar and sugar in a medium saucepan. Bring to the boil then simmer, covered, for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Remove from heat, add butter and stir until melted. Transfer to a large bowl and cool to room temperature.
Sift together wholemeal flour and baking powder into a bowl, returning husks from flour to the bowl. Stir the beaten egg and flour into the apricot mixture and turn into the prepared tin.
Bake for 50- 60 minutes, until cooked. If necessary cover with tin foil part way through baking to prevent overbrowning of the crust.
